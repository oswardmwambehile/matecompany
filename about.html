Company Introduction

Mate Limited is a Tanzanian company that produces Mwamba Vodka and Mwamba Gin. All are made and produced by our competitive staff, ensuring the quality of our products remains stable and competitive in our markets. The up-to-date machinery that ensures constant supply to the market makes Mati Super Brands Limited favorable to consumers.

Our History

Mate Limited is a company that has come a long way in customer satisfaction, providing the best quality products to fit the desire of the market. Here at Mate Company Limited we produce the best brands of spirits that are fit for human consumption from our world-class machinery that ensures the best product is made and the level of quality and perfection is maintained. We as a factory ensure all standards are met as per the government regulations.

Our Mission

Our goal is to cover the Dodoma Market and its surrounding regions to ensure the availability of our product and expand to the rest of the country like Dodoma, Dar–es–Salaam, Mara, Mbeya, Mwanza, Tabora etc.

Products that are produced by Mate company are Mwamba Vodka and Mwamba Gin. All are made and produced by our competitive staff which ensures the quality of our products remains stable and competitive in our markets. The quality of our products is ensured by top-of-class staff that allows the blending of the spirit and gin to its best perfection.
